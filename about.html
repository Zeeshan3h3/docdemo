Prof. (Dr.) S.C. Santra

M.B.B.S., DGO., MS. (Cal)

Gynecologist & Obstetrician

✅ Reg. No. 35322 (WBMC)

Prof. (Dr.) S.C. Santra is a highly esteemed Gynecologist and Obstetrician practicing in Picnic Garden, Kolkata. With a distinguished career spanning over three decades, he has dedicated his life to women's health and wellbeing.

He served as the Medical Superintendent cum Vice-Principal at NRS Medical College & Hospital, Kolkata — one of the most prestigious medical institutions in Eastern India. During his tenure, he played a pivotal role in improving maternal healthcare services and training the next generation of medical professionals.

His areas of expertise include pregnancy care, management of high-risk pregnancies, PCOS treatment, menstrual disorders, fertility consultation, and comprehensive women's health checkups. He is known for his patient-centric approach, where he takes the time to listen, explain, and provide personalized treatment plans.
