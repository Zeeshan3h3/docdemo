Privacy Policy

How we collect, use, and protect your information

Home › Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 2026

1. Information We Collect

When you use the appointment booking form on this website, we collect your name, phone number, preferred appointment date and time, and any health-related information you voluntarily share in the message field.

2. How We Use Your Information

Your information is used solely to schedule and confirm your appointment with the clinic. It is shared via WhatsApp with clinic staff to process your booking. We do not sell, trade, or share your personal information with any third party for marketing purposes.

3. Data Retention

Appointment booking data is retained only as long as necessary for the purpose of your consultation. Clinic records are maintained in accordance with applicable Indian medical regulations.

4. Your Rights (DPDP Act 2023)

Under India's Digital Personal Data Protection Act 2023, you have the right to access your personal data, request correction of inaccurate data, and request deletion of your data. To exercise these rights, contact us at swapancoomar@yahoo.com or call +91 70019 99079.

5. WhatsApp Communication

When you click "Book via WhatsApp", your message is transmitted directly through WhatsApp's platform, subject to WhatsApp's own privacy policy. Please review WhatsApp's Privacy Policy for more information.

6. Cookies & Local Storage

This website uses only a minimal local storage preference to remember your language setting (English or Bengali). No tracking cookies or third-party advertising cookies are used.
